EqualVoice

EqualVoice is a web-based platform dedicated to raising awareness about social inequality and championing digital inclusion. The project was born from a belief that technology should be a bridge, not a barrier — giving every individual, regardless of background or circumstance, a fair opportunity to access information, resources, and opportunities online. Through thoughtful design and purposeful content, EqualVoice amplifies underrepresented voices and works toward building a more equitable and inclusive digital experience for all communities.

HTML CSS JavaScript

DNSC Map Navigation System

A campus navigation system designed to help visitors and new students find their way around the DNSC campus with ease. The system features an interactive map that displays a guided route — highlighted with a red directional line — from the user's current location to their chosen destination. Each key building and landmark is accompanied by a photo, so users can visually recognize the facility before they arrive, making the campus experience less overwhelming and more welcoming for first-timers.
